# Quant v0.1.0 — Request for Comments & Collaboration

**Tag:** `quant-v0.1.0`  
**Date:** June 2026

## Summary

First public release of **The Quant (ℚ): A Standard for Measuring Computational
Energy Efficiency** — a substrate-independent, Landauer-normalized unit of
computational energy efficiency. Published as an open RFC alongside DUCP; DUCP
v0.2.0 is the first reference adopter (DP-0001, `spec/09`).
