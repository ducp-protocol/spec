# DUCP Contributor License Agreement (Draft)

> **Draft — not yet in force, and not legal advice.** This is a plain-language
> draft of the agreement the project intends to require from contributors. It
> has **not** been reviewed by an attorney. Before DUCP accepts contributions
> under it, the author intends to have it (and the trademark and license
> position) reviewed and finalized by counsel. Do not rely on it as legal
> advice; consult your own lawyer regarding your situation.

## Purpose

DUCP is stewarded by a single author through its pre-1.0 phase. To keep
ownership of the protocol unified — so that it can be licensed, defended, and
(at v1.0) opened under one clear set of terms — contributions are accepted only
under this agreement. By contributing, you agree to the following.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Author"** means Pawan Singh, the steward and copyright holder of DUCP.
- **"You"** means the individual or legal entity making a Contribution.
- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship — text, diagrams,
  code, designs, or other material — that You intentionally submit to the
  project (for example, via pull request, issue, or email) for inclusion in DUCP.

## 2. Assignment of copyright

You assign to the Author all right, title, and interest in the copyright of Your
Contributions, worldwide and for the full term of protection. The Author may use,
reproduce, modify, publish, sublicense, relicense, and distribute the
Contributions and derivative works, under any license the Author chooses,
including future open-source or commercial licenses.

## 3. License back to You

The Author grants You a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license
to use, reproduce, modify, and distribute Your own Contributions for any purpose.
You are never restricted from using the work You created.

You grant the Author and all recipients of DUCP a perpetual, worldwide,
royalty-free, irrevocable (except as stated below) license under any patent
claims You own or control that are necessarily infringed by Your Contribution, to

terminate.

## 5. Moral rights

To the extent permitted by applicable law, You waive, and agree not to assert,
any moral rights in Your Contributions against the Author or its licensees. Where
such rights cannot be waived, You agree not to enforce them in a way that would
frustrate the licenses granted above.

## 6. Your representations

You represent that:

- each Contribution is Your original creation, or You have the right to submit it
  and to grant the rights above;
- if Your employer has rights to intellectual property You create, You have
  received permission to make the Contribution on behalf of that employer, or
  the employer has waived such rights for the Contribution; and
- Your Contribution does not, to Your knowledge, violate any third party's
  rights.

## 7. No obligation

The Author is under no obligation to use, include, or maintain any Contribution.
Contributions are provided "as is," without warranty of any kind, except as
stated in Section 6.

## 8. Entity contributions

If You are agreeing on behalf of a company or other legal entity, You represent
that You are authorized to bind that entity, and "You" includes that entity and
its affiliates.

## 9. Miscellaneous

This agreement is the entire agreement concerning Contributions and supersedes
prior understandings on the subject. If a provision is held unenforceable, the
remainder stays in effect. The governing law and venue will be specified in the
finalized agreement.

## How to sign

Until an automated process (such as a CLA bot or a signed `CLA-signatures` file)
is in place, You may agree by stating, in Your pull request or by email to
mr.singhpawan@gmail.com:

> "I have read and agree to the DUCP Contributor License Agreement."

with Your name and the date.
